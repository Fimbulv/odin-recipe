Hamburger

Description

This recipe will make a mouth watering hamburger. Simple and quick recipe which means no stress

Ingredients

  • 2 LB Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Goya Seasoning
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • American Cheese
  • Onion Slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Hamburger Bun

Steps

  1. In a bowl,combine the meat, seasonings listed. Crack eggs and add, mix well until all is combined
  2. Roll meat up into spheres,lay flat to create burger patty
  3. Add oil into pan, heat up on medium
  4. Add patty onto pan, flip every 90 seconds until burger is cooked evenly (middle of patty should have darkening)
  5. OPTIONAL. Add slice of American Cheese to the top of patty, lower heat and cover with lid until cheese has melted
  6. Chop up the Tomato, onion and lettuce
  7. Add all ingredients into bun
