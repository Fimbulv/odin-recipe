Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

This recipe will provide a basic garlic bread option. Although it is basic, it packs a punch with its delicious flavor and low prep time

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French Bread
  • 4 Whole Garlic Heads
  • 1 Stick of butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Parmesian Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 Fahrenheit
  2. Place all garlic heads on aluminum foil, drizzle olive oil lightly and wrap up the garlic tightly
  3. Once the oven is ready,add the garlic in the oven and let it roast for one hour
  4. Remove the garlic from the oven and allow time to cool off
  5. In a bowl, mush the garlic adding the butter,basil, Oregano, and Parmesian cheese and heavily mix
  6. Cut the loaf of bread in two, spread the paste and distribute evenly thoughout the bread
  7. Add the bread back in the oven for another 12 minutes,allow to cool off after removing from oven
