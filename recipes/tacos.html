Tacos

Description

These tacos are authenticly made using basic kitchen ingredients that anybody can use. Not only is there simplicity, but it also provides an incredible authenticly mexican cuisine

Ingredients

  • 2LB Ground Beef
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt (Sea Salt)
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Goya Seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Chopped Onion
  • Corn Tortilla

Steps

  1. Remove ground beef from the packagaging, add into the bowl
  2. In the bowl, add together the seasonings, make sure you're not being reserved dunk everything in there or else it won't taste good
  3. Add oil to pan on medium heat, after 3 minutes of warming up add the beef into the pan
  4. Lightly mix the meat in the pan,allow for it to cook while occasionally mixing
  5. While the meat is heating, chop the onion and cilantro into finley chopped pieces
  6. Keep mixing until meat is golden brown and there is no liquid seeping through the bottom,kill the heat and remove from stove
  7. Warm up tortillas (either in the microwave or through the oven)
  8. Serve by adding meat into tortillas, add onion and cilantro by taste
